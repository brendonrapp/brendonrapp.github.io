In My Image...

Oct 4th, 2006

She complained when we reached the end of Battlestar Galactica season 1 box set and found that I didn’t have season 2 handy.

She was annoyed when she came over and found that my TiVo has not been recording the late night South Park reruns on UPN the past week.

Because of her, I can not watch Studio 60 or Entourage when they’ve been freshly taped – I must wait for her to come over and watch too.

Every Monday Night, we go out to a pizza place and watch football. She now watches some on Sunday on her own as well – not a fanatic yet but her football viewing and knowledge increases each season.

Yes, it’s taken 5 years, but slowly and surely Stacey’s tastes are being molded and improved. :)
